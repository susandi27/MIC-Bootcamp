Ingredient Chosing and Making process of CHOCOLATE.

Cleaning and Sorting

To ensure quality, the beans are inspected, cleaned and sorted.

Grinding

TThe cacao nibs are then transferred to a stone melangeur, a grinding machine used in the traditional chocolate making process, after the cacao beans undergo several processes.

Tempering/Moulding

After several processes, before being transfered into mould, the chocolate is tempered.
